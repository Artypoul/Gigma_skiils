# Page structure library

Choose structure by page type and intent. Do not copy all sections into every page.

## Homepage

1. Hero: what, for whom, outcome, CTA.
2. Problem/situation.
3. Product/service overview.
4. Main benefit pillars.
5. How it works or mechanism.
6. Use cases or segments.
7. Proof: logos, case, numbers, examples.
8. Resources or next pages.
9. FAQ / objections.
10. Final CTA.

## Landing page

1. Hero with offer.
2. Problem and cost of current way.
3. Solution/mechanism.
4. Benefits.
5. Proof.
6. How it works.
7. Risk reducers.
8. FAQ.
9. Final CTA.

## Service page

1. Service promise.
2. Who it is for.
3. Problems solved.
4. Scope and deliverables.
5. Process.
6. Cases/proof.
7. Pricing/scope notes if possible.
8. FAQ.
9. Consultation/request CTA.

## SaaS product page

1. Product/category clarity.
2. Core workflow.
3. Feature groups as outcomes.
4. Screenshots/demo notes.
5. Integrations/security if relevant.
6. Proof.
7. Pricing/trial/demo path.
8. FAQ.

## Comparison page

1. Who is comparing and why.
2. Summary recommendation.
3. Decision criteria.
4. Comparison table.
5. Where each option fits.
6. Why choose us for [segment].
7. Proof.
8. Migration/switching notes.
9. CTA.

## Pricing page

1. Clear plans or pricing principle.
2. Who each plan is for.
3. Feature/value comparison.
4. Objection handling: hidden costs, cancellation, support.
5. Proof/risk reducers.
6. FAQ.
7. CTA.

## SEO guide/article

1. Quick answer.
2. Context/definition.
3. Framework or steps.
4. Examples.
5. Mistakes.
6. Template/checklist.
7. Product tie-in.
8. Related links.
