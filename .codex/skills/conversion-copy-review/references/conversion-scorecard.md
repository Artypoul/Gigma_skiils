# Conversion scorecard

Score each criterion from 1 to 5.

| Score | Meaning |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Broken or missing |
| 2 | Present but weak/confusing |
| 3 | Acceptable but generic |
| 4 | Clear and useful |
| 5 | Strong, specific, and proven |

## Priority definitions

- P1: blocks understanding, trust, or conversion.
- P2: reduces persuasion or creates friction.
- P3: style, polish, or secondary improvement.

## First-screen test

Can a new visitor answer in 5 seconds?

1. What is this?
2. Who is it for?
3. What result does it promise?
4. Why believe it?
5. What should I do next?

If not, start fixes with hero and message hierarchy.
